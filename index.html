Welcome! The goal of this tool is simple: gather as many of the most commonly-used library metadata crosswalks in one place as possible. Simply choose a standard from each menu and hit submit, and you will be redirected to a table with the relevant crosswalk conventions. If a crosswalk requires more information than a standard table can provide, the page will include relevant links and notes with more details.

To learn more about the site, to follow its development progress, or to find out more about its creator, see the relevant links above. You will also find links to other resources that catalogers might find handy under Other Tools.
